Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire adventure and create unforgettable experiences on wild rivers. We are dedicated to offering whitewater rafting expeditions that combine thrill, safety, and environmental respect, ensuring that every journey leaves you with lifelong memories.

History

Our story began in 1995 when a small group of passionate explorers decided to tame the untamed rivers of Patagonia. With nothing but a shared love for adventure and the great outdoors, they organized local trips that soon captured the imagination of thrill-seekers across the region.

Over the years, our company has grown from humble beginnings to an internationally recognized leader in whitewater rafting. Today, we blend innovative techniques with a commitment to sustainability, ensuring that every expedition not only excites but also respects the natural environment.
